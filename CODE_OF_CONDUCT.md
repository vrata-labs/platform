# Code of Conduct

Noah uses a simple contributor standard: be direct, respectful, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat contributors and users with respect.
- Prefer factual technical feedback over personal criticism.
- Assume good intent, but resolve disagreements with evidence and tests.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project.
- Respect privacy and do not disclose private information or credentials.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Deliberately introducing insecure code, hidden telemetry, or credential leaks.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.
